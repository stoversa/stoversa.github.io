About Me

The Rust Belt's Native Son, Sam spent three years as a reference librarian in North Dakota (fun fact: the least visited state), before returning to the South for milder weather. He now helps librarians everyday by providing technical support for several essential products.


When he is not working with librarians or coding, Sam spends his time cooking, reading in libraries, and sipping coffee in Durham. He also collects too many books, a professional hazard from his library days.
